# My-Space

My Space is a journaling web app designed to help you manage stress and express your thoughts in a safe, creative way. Inspired by mental health research, the app lets you click on animated planets to open small, personal writing spaces—your own corner of the galaxy to reflect, vent, or just breathe. Whether you're feeling overwhelmed or just need to clear your mind, My Space gives you a private space to write, save, or erase your thoughts. No pressure, no judgment—just you and your words.
